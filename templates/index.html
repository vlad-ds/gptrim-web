Guide

There are two ways to reduce your GPT prompt size. By reducing characters, you can fit more text into a single chat message. By reducing tokens, you can save money on API calls and increase the context window size.

Stopwords removal reduces both characters and tokens.

Stemming and space removal reduce characters, but increase token usage.

You should experiment until you find the right solution!
